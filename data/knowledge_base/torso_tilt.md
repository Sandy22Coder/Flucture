# Sideways Torso Tilt

Common signs:
- Rib cage shifting to one side
- Weight not balanced evenly

Possible consequences:
- Uneven trunk loading
- Hip or lower-back discomfort
- Poor balance during exercise

Corrective actions:
- Balance weight through both feet
- Keep ribs stacked over pelvis
- Pause and reset when fatigue starts

Remedies:
- Side plank regressions
- Dead bug variations
- Standing wall alignment drill

Safety:
- Stop if lower-back pain increases
- Reduce exercise intensity when form breaks down
