# Lateral Neck Tilt

Common signs:
- Ear drifting toward one shoulder
- Neck tilted during desk work or standing

Possible consequences:
- One-sided neck soreness
- Muscle imbalance over time
- Reduced neck comfort during long study sessions

Corrective actions:
- Center the head over the sternum
- Keep both shoulders level
- Adjust monitor and chair alignment

Remedies:
- Gentle side-neck stretch
- Mirror-based posture correction
- Scapular stability work

Safety:
- Avoid aggressive neck stretching
- Seek help for dizziness or nerve symptoms
