# Shoulder Imbalance

Common signs:
- One shoulder staying higher than the other
- Uneven upper-trap tension

Possible consequences:
- Neck tightness
- Shoulder fatigue
- Compensated movement patterns

Corrective actions:
- Relax the raised shoulder
- Keep both elbows moving symmetrically
- Check desk and bag habits

Remedies:
- Upper-trapezius stretch
- Scapular retraction drill
- Light rowing exercise

Safety:
- Stop if shoulder pain becomes sharp
- Do not load overhead movement during pain
