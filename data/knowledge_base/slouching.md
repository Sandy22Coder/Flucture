# Slouching / Rounded Upper Back

Common signs:
- Torso leaning forward
- Chest collapsing during sitting or exercise

Possible consequences:
- Mid-back fatigue
- Lower breathing efficiency
- Shoulder discomfort

Corrective actions:
- Stack shoulders over hips
- Lift the breastbone slightly
- Take posture breaks every 30 to 45 minutes

Remedies:
- Thoracic extension over a towel
- Band pull-aparts
- Seated posture reset drill

Safety:
- Avoid forcing the lower back into pain
- Use slow controlled range only
