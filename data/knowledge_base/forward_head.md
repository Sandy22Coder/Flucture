# Forward Head Posture

Common signs:
- Chin drifting ahead of the shoulders
- Neck muscles staying tense for long periods

Possible consequences:
- Neck stiffness
- Upper back tightness
- Headaches after desk work

Corrective actions:
- Gentle chin tucks
- Keep screen at eye level
- Reset shoulders down and back

Remedies:
- Chin tucks for 8 to 10 reps
- Doorway chest stretch for 30 seconds
- Wall slides with slow control

Safety:
- Stop if pain shoots down the arm
- Seek help for numbness or persistent pain
